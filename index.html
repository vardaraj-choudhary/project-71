Campus life

Indus valley world school -CAMPUS

indus valley world school is a Central Board of Secondary Education school located in ajoy Nagar, kolkata, India.The pre-primary and early years learning programme at CPS is a system where the tenets of Montessori education are scientifically combined with tried and tested teaching methods, focussing on language development and logical thinking, for children to start their first steps into the education system.

ART CLASSROOM

To kindle a conducive learning environment on par with the best institutions worldwide, indus valley world School has spared no effort or investment to ensure that the best infrastructure is made available to students to enable them to pursue their education with ease and comfort.

Smart Class – Interactive Digital classroom:

indus valley world School was one of the first schools in India to have smart boards in all the classrooms and also a 5.1 theatre system in all classrooms.

Future 50 schools shaping success

Indus valley world school has etched a covetous place as one among the top 50 Schools in India, Shaping Success in the 2017 ranking conducted by the Future 50 - Fortune Magazine. Over 250 schools from 60+ cities all over the country participated in this ranking.

Center of excellence for kids
Center of excellence for kids

ATAL tinkering labs take CPS one step closer to the vision ‘Cultivate one Million children in India as Neoteric Innovators’.They are the pivots of innovation that foster imagination and creativity in young minds.

Sports & games
Sports & games

Students are segregated into four houses namely Alphard House, Canopus House, Diadem House and Plasma House. Intra-mural games and sports are conducted to promote healthy competitions among the four houses where the adrenaline is gushed up as they take part in all events energetically.
